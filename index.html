From zero to a live bot in 5 steps

No installs, no QR scanning headaches. Link your number, then control everything from WhatsApp itself.

HOW IT WORKS
Getting started
1
Tap Pair WhatsApp Now below to open the secure pairing page
2
Enter your number with country code and generate your 8-digit code
3
Open WhatsApp → ⋮ Menu → Linked Devices → Link a Device
4
Choose "Link with phone number instead" and enter the code
5
That's it — the bot is live on your number instantly 🚀
Using the bot
⚡
Send .menu in any chat to see every available command
🤖
Mention the bot or reply to its messages in a group to chat with AI
👑
Owners get extra admin commands — group tools, scheduler, broadcasts
🔒
Your session stays private — only you control your linked number
